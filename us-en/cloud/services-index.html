Expertise

Embarking on the adventure of business transformation brings excitement to your entire organization. Yet, the real thrill awaits as you reach your destination in the cloud, unlocking a world where you can dream on a grand scale, move with unprecedented speed, and soar to greater heights than ever before.

Zetatech Cloud Services drive enterprise transformation by employing innovative cloud applications. We integrate business expertise, cohesive business, and technology services, adopting a people-centric approach to assist businesses in their progression to the cloud and beyond. Our diverse range of capabilities encompasses Application Modernization and migration, Cloud Analytics & AI, Cloud Business Transformation, Cloud Infrastructure and engineering, Cloud Native Development, and various other services.

The upcoming phase of the resilience-focused cloud journey

In response to recent disruptions, organizations have swiftly embraced cloud and digital transformation, enhancing their resilience. However, the next imperative is to reimagine and reinforce integration through a unified cloud strategy. This will unlock delayed value and optimize costs for greater efficiency.

Cloud consulting is instrumental in empowering companies to harness the full potential of cloud computing, addressing challenges and unlocking a multitude of benefits. Our team of seasoned cloud consultants is dedicated to guiding you through a seamless transition to your preferred cloud platform. Beyond migration, our comprehensive suite of services spans cloud development, integration, and strategic redesign, bringing forth a host of advantages:

Scalability

Effortlessly scale your infrastructure up or down based on business demands, ensuring optimal resource utilization and cost efficiency.

Flexibility

Adapt quickly to changing business needs by leveraging the flexibility of cloud services, enabling dynamic adjustments to computing resources.

Cost Optimization

Optimize your IT spending with pay-as-you-go models, eliminating the need for heavy upfront investments and enabling a more predictable cost structure.

Enhanced Collaboration

Foster collaboration among teams, regardless of geographical locations, by providing secure and centralized access to shared resources and applications.

Security and Compliance

Leverage robust security features and compliance measures offered by leading cloud providers, ensuring the protection of sensitive data and adherence to industry regulations..

Innovation Acceleration

Access cutting-edge technologies and tools, facilitating innovation and enabling your organization to stay ahead in a rapidly evolving digital landscape.

Business Continuity

Mitigate risks associated with data loss or system failures by implementing robust backup and disaster recovery solutions, ensuring seamless business continuity.

Global Reach

Expand your business's global footprint effortlessly by leveraging the widespread network of data centers offered by leading cloud service providers, ensuring low-latency access for users around the world.

The next leg of the cloud journey to resilience

Organizations have responded to recent disruption with rapid cloud and digital transformation. While they have boosted resilience, they must now reinvent for greater integration under a cohesive cloud strategy to realize delayed value and tighten costs.

  1. A tipping point to full cloud value
  2. Untangling value from chaos via cloud
  3. Build your core for breakthrough resilience
  4. Cloud security amid uncertainty

With more than 86% of companies reporting an increase in cloud initiatives since 2020, only 42% report fully achieving the outcomes they expected. The most elusive of them all? Cost savings, which take a starring role amid economic uncertainty. 

Caught between the promise of large-scale cloud transformation and the complexity that must be addressed to realize it, including rethinking operating models, talent, etc., organizations face a tipping point: They've picked the low-hanging fruit of cloud but must now double down to achieve its full value. How do we know? Because the ones who achieve outcomes more often are heavy adopters who see cloud as a continuously evolving journey, not a destination.

Naturally, the further you go, the more complex the challenges. Understand the barriers to cloud value and learn five practices that will help you move beyond them.

In times of uncertainty, the most agile businesses are the ones that survive and thrive. What do they all have in common? High interoperability.

Interoperability is when enterprise applications seamlessly interact and exchange data, connecting technology, people and processes to improve collaboration and decision-making. 

According to our research, companies with high interoperability grew revenue six times faster than peers with low interoperability and unlocked an additional five percentage points in annual revenue growth. 

Replatforming to cloud brings your organization unprecedented interoperability. By modernizing operations on a common platform, you can run, experiment and innovate with greater speed and agility to lower costs, optimize spend and improve resilience.

Learn more about how to level up interoperability for long-term value with cloud.

In light of the current economic climate, businesses are redefining their imperatives around cost efficiency, growth and agility. They know the pace of change calls for continuous reinvention to remain resilient, but many find themselves hamstringed by a familiar foe.

Legacy technology is one of the biggest agility killers, limiting your ability to change and innovate. To address that, organizations must build a strong digital core around cloud, AI and metaverse. Together, these technologies not only help you continuously reinvent to weather uncertainty—they also help you capitalize on opportunities along the way through breakthrough innovation.

Accenture's Chief Technology Officer Paul Daugherty illuminates the opportunities these three megatrends ignite when they combine to form your core.

With cloud serving a key component in a strong digital core, it is critical to ensure your journey is guided by security at every turn.

Remember: Cloud is a continuum of technologies and capabilities, each with their own exposure profiles. Your approach to security must be just as complex and wide-reaching. That means embracing a "zero-trust" mindset across your network.
