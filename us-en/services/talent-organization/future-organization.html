Our passion is to design great organizations

To deliver on ambitious business strategies, our clients come to us with challenges of:

  • Spurring growth and innovation
  • Reducing complexity and cost
  • Integrating acquisitions and managing divestments
  • Retaining enterprise agility, while scaling and leveraging assets
  • Gaining the benefits of digital transformation
  • Delivering on sustainability commitments

Our work is the foundation of Total Enterprise Reinvention. We work with companies who are industry leaders and changers. Our human-centered approach ensures that we design aligned, healthy, and dynamic organizations that create the environment for talent to thrive.

Accenture’s Operating Model & Organization Design practice was expanded by the acquisition of Kates Kesler, developers of the leading methodology in the field. Together, our frameworks and tools are the global standard and are backed by our deep analytics capability and industry expertise. Our global team is uniquely positioned to co-create clients’ future organizations.

Capabilities

Enterprise Operating Models

We take a strategy-led and data-driven systems approach to enterprise design. Our work delivers the right balance of agility and scale, and growth and efficiency required by today’s complex business strategies.

Functions and Shared Services

We have the experts and benchmarks to guide design of a single function as well as complete global business services architecture. We bring technology and operations insights to provide a full suite of structure, location, staffing, and automation options.​

Activation

The design work isn’t complete until leaders are making decisions that lead to new outcomes. We ensure the right connections and conversations are wired into the organization and leaders are practicing and modeling new ways of work.​

Optimization and Innovation

We can help you build the mechanisms to measure design impacts and monitor the health of the organization. Proactive adjustments, based on robust data-based insights, allows for continuous evolution and avoids disruptive restructuring events.

Digitally powered business models require organizations that can deliver better decision-making at the center and the edge. Today's organization models must deliver both agility and scale. An investment in organization design is as foundational as an investment in technology.​

The Kates Kesler methodology

Accenture’s Operating Model & Organization Design practice is built on Kates Kesler’s industry-leading methodology and proven approach for designing and activating organizations to deliver complex business strategies.

  1. ABOUT KATES KESLER
  2. THOUGHT LEADERSHIP

Accenture acquired the Kates Kesler boutique to serve as the foundation for building the world’s leading organization design practice.

Founders Amy Kates and Greg Kesler have developed a tested and proven methodology, based on the pioneering work of Jay Galbraith and refined in over 20 years of application.

Kates Kesler served as consultants to Accenture CEO, Julie Sweet, to support the design and activation of the Accenture Next Generation Growth Model:

“To get the organizational design right, I turned to some of the strongest leaders in the field—Greg, Amy, and Michele at Kates Kesler. It became apparent that not only would they be critical to achieving our goal, but their specialized skills would be an increasing need for our clients’ transformations.” said Sweet.

The Kates Kesler methodology is now enhanced by the analytics capability, industry expertise, and deep experience of Accenture practitioners who, together, form Accenture’s Operating Model and Organizational Design practice.

Greg Kesler and Amy Kates’ books serve as guides to C-Suite leaders, HR professionals, and as textbooks in dozens of MBA and graduate programs around the world. They document Kates Kesler’s frameworks and methods including:

  • A strategy-led, systems (Star Model) approach to designing human behavior at scale.
  • Matching organization to the business model (closely and loosely related portfolios).
  • Designing the vertical and horizontal organization for an effective matrix.
  • Process, metrics, and governance to drive faster, better decisions.
  • Activation of connections, conversations, and leader behaviors to bring organizational intentions to life.

The practice continues to invest in research to create practical insights and tools for clients to meet evolving business challenges. Their process ensures that leadership teams deeply understand the choices they are making and are aligned in how they will manage through change.
