A leader in mining operational process optimization

Accenture has been named a Leader in the IDC MarketScape: Worldwide Professional Services Firms for Mining Operational Process Optimization 2023 Vendor Assessment.

This IDC MarketScape study evaluates the capabilities and strategies of IT and professional services firms providing services to mining companies across their worldwide operations. The following Accenture strengths are noted:

  • Considered a trusted and preferred partner for many of its customers due to its ability to create multidisciplinary teams combining its capabilities in strategy, digital skills, project management, BPO and partnerships.
  • Consistently invests in R&D to foster digital and business innovation and to nurture new solutions and ways of working that solve operational problems for its mining clients.
  • Has scale in the mining sector and a strong ecosystem of strategy, technology and academic partners that can serve the mining value chain for its clients and deliver transformative operational and business outcomes.
READ MORE

IDC MarketScape vendor analysis model is designed to provide an overview of the competitive fitness of ICT suppliers in a given market. The research methodology utilizes a rigorous scoring methodology based on both qualitative and quantitative criteria that results in a single graphical illustration of each vendor’s position within a given market. The Capabilities score measures vendor product, go-to-market and business execution in the short term. The Strategy score measures alignment of vendor strategies with customer requirements in a 3-5-year timeframe. Vendor market share is represented by the size of the circles.
