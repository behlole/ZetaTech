Tech Vision 2023

Our generative AI experience

Next-level innovation and reinvention: In a fast moving, emerging space, generative AI will transform work across industries. The impact can be seen across the enterprise from customer service to hyper-automation to knowledge management set to be transformed.

  1. Simplify data with a knowledge management platform
  2. AI platform to demystify the Justice system
  3. Automation creates a smoother customer experience
  4. Helping a European banking group to search better
  5. Foundation models for a platform leader

One of the largest oil and gas companies has mountains of data and generates more every day. We’re helping the company more easily use and access this data with multi-modal data handling, cognitive search, and semantic modelling, as well as the latest generative AI innovations from Microsoft Azure OpenAI. Together, we’re creating a foundation for organization-wide understanding of data that automates knowledge gathering and makes search easy.

We’re working with Spain’s Ministry of Justice to simplify how critical information about judicial processes is accessed, using large language models (LLMs). Deployed on Microsoft Cloud, we’ve designed Delfos, an AI-powered search engine for judges, prosecutors, defense lawyers, and citizens. Delfos gives these people a quick, efficient way to learn about judicial processes, by finding and simplifying information buried within hundreds of thousands of complex documents.

We’re working with a multinational bank to transform how it manages high volumes of emails related to trade processing. Thousands of emails are received every day regarding the resolution of exceptions and the status of event processing. We are leveraging a generative AI solution that is built to review, understand and automatically direct large numbers of emails daily to relevant teams with a high degree of accuracy. It also drafts responses for the human operators to enhance productivity and impact. Our work will help reduce manual effort and risk, boost worker efficiency, and improve customer satisfaction.

We’re working with a European banking group to transform its knowledge base and make it easier for users to find information. Built with Microsoft’s Azure architecture and a GPT-3 large language model (LLM), the application quickly searches vast collections of documents to find the correct answers to employees’ questions. We’re also helping upskill its employees, so that they can scale data use across the banking group, supporting its three-year innovation plan.

We’re helping a global platform leader enter the generative AI space with its own proprietary solution. Backed by the power of one of the largest cloud-computing platforms, these new models are pre-trained on contextual, industry-specific data sets, including both text and images. These models will accelerate processes by answering questions and providing automated content across HR and IT support, product design, document management and much more.
